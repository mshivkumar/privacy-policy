# Privacy Policy for Daily Task App Effective Date: March 3, 2026 Daily Task App ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the Daily Task App mobile application (the "App"). ## 1. Information We Collect We may collect the following categories of information: 1. Account and Identity Data - Name, email address, and account identifiers when you sign up or sign in. - Google account basic profile data when you use Google Sign-In. 2. Task and App Content - Task details you create (for example: title, description, reminders, checklist items, category, and status). - Optional media attached to tasks (images and audio notes). - Optional location reminder details (such as selected places/geofences). 3. Device and Technical Data - App performance and crash information (via Firebase Crashlytics). - Usage analytics/events (via Firebase Analytics). - Device/app instance identifiers and notification tokens (for push notifications). 4. Permissions-Based Data (Optional) - Location data, including background location, only for location-based reminders. - Microphone/audio data for voice notes or speech-to-text features. - Camera/media access for attaching images to tasks. - Notification permission for reminders and alerts. ## 2. How We Use Information We use data to: - Create and manage your account. - Provide core app functionality (task management, reminders, sync where enabled). - Send push/local notifications for reminders. - Enable location-based reminders, including geofence triggers. - Enable optional voice, recording, and image attachment features. - Improve reliability, diagnose crashes, and improve the App experience. - Protect against abuse, fraud, and security issues. ## 3. Guest Mode If you use the App as a guest, your task data is stored locally on your device and is not tied to a signed-in account unless you later create/sign into an account and migrate data. ## 4. Sharing of Information We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers strictly to operate the App, including: - Google Firebase services (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Messaging, Analytics, Crashlytics). - Google Sign-In. These providers may process data according to their own privacy terms. ## 5. Data Retention We retain data for as long as needed to provide the App and related services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. - Account-linked cloud data may be retained until account deletion or user request. - Local guest-mode data remains on your device until you remove it or uninstall the App. ## 6. Your Choices and Controls You can: - Update your profile name inside the App. - Turn permissions (location, microphone, camera, notifications) on/off in system settings. - Use guest mode instead of creating an account. - Request deletion of your account/data by contacting us (see Contact section). ## 7. Security We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed. ## 8. Children’s Privacy The App is not directed to children under 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
